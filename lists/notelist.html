src/samples_from_md/main.f90We can show the histograms of the force magnitudes in FeV, together with the relative error in forces. We compare forces obtained from BOMD to the small displacement method. The small displacement method samples forces at magnitudes where the relative errors is about two orders of magnitude larger than at a thermally relevant state. So not only do we better describe the physics at high temperature, we do it in a numerically superior manner.
